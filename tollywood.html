★ ★ ★ TOLLYWOOD MOVIES ★ ★ ★

Tollywood refers to the Telugu-language film industry primarily based in Hyderabad, India. Known for its grand action scenes, strong storytelling, and entertaining music, Tollywood has gained international recognition in recent years. Its movies are widely loved by audiences across India and around the world.

LEO

CAST-Vijay, Sanjay Dutt, Trisha Krishnan, Arjun Sarja

DESCRIPTION - Parthiban is a quiet café owner living a peaceful life with his family in Himachal Pradesh. His life suddenly changes when he bravely fights off a group of dangerous criminals in his café. The incident makes him famous, but it also attracts powerful gangsters who believe he is actually Leo Das, a feared gangster from their past. As dark secrets begin to surface, Parthiban must face his past and protect his family from powerful enemies.

PUSHPA:THE RISE

CAST-Allu Arjun, Rashmika Mandanna, Fahadh Faasil

DESCRIPTION - Pushpa Raj is a fearless laborer who rises in the dangerous world of red sandalwood smuggling in the forests of Andhra Pradesh. With his intelligence, courage, and unique style, he slowly climbs the ranks of the illegal trade. However, his growing power brings him into conflict with powerful enemies and a determined police officer who is ready to stop his empire.

ALA VAIKUNTHAPURRAMULOO

CAST-Allu Arjun, Pooja Hegde, Tabu, Murali Sharma

DESCRIPTION - Bantu grows up constantly facing criticism from his strict father and feeling unwanted in his own home. Later he discovers a shocking secret that he was switched at birth with the son of a rich family. Determined to protect his real family from danger, Bantu enters the wealthy household and uses his courage and intelligence to solve problems and bring happiness to everyone.

SITA RAMAM

CAST-Dulquer Salmaan, Mrunal Thakur, Rashmika Mandanna

DESCRIPTION - The story follows Lieutenant Ram, a kind-hearted soldier serving in the Indian Army, who receives a mysterious love letter from a woman named Sita. Their beautiful romance grows through letters and secret meetings. Years later, a young woman sets out on a journey to deliver Ram’s final letter to Sita, uncovering a touching love story filled with sacrifice, loyalty, and destiny.

SALAAR: CEASEFIRE

CAST-Prabhas, Prithviraj Sukumaran, Shruti Haasan

DESCRIPTION - Set in the powerful and violent city of Khansaar, the story follows Deva, a mysterious and fearless man who becomes involved in a deadly power struggle. When his childhood friend Varadha faces threats from rival factions, Deva rises to protect him and challenge powerful enemies. Filled with intense action and political conflicts, the story explores friendship, loyalty, and betrayal.

KAANTARA

CAST- Rishab Shetty, Sapthami Gowda, Kishore

DESCRIPTION -Set in a coastal village in Karnataka, the story revolves around Shiva, a carefree young man who lives close to nature and local traditions. When a conflict arises between villagers and government officials over forest land, ancient beliefs and spiritual forces begin to influence events. As tensions grow, Shiva must face his destiny and protect the traditions and faith of his community.
